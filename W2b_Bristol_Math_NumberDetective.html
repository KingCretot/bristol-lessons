🎯Rounding Squad — Estimation

Detectives don’t always need the EXACT number — sometimes a smart guess is enough. Rounding to the nearest ten: look at the ones digit. 0-4 rounds DOWN, 5-9 rounds UP.
🔎 Detective Example
📍 Round 67 to the nearest ten: ones digit is 7 (that’s 5 or more) → round UP → 70
📍 Round 43 to the nearest ten: ones digit is 3 (less than 5) → round DOWN → 40
