➕ New Skill

3-Digit Addition with Regrouping

Adding hundreds works just like 2-digit adding — we just have one more column! Add ones first, then tens, then hundreds. 🏠

📚 Watch how 247 + 156 works:

Step 1 — Ones: 7 + 6 = 13. Write the 3, carry the 1.

Step 2 — Tens: 1 + 4 + 5 = 10. Write the 0, carry the 1.

Step 3 — Hundreds: 1 + 2 + 1 = 4.

¹¹  247 +156  403

✨ Answer: 403!

➖ New Skill

3-Digit Subtraction with Regrouping

When the top digit is too small, we trade 1 from the next column — just like with 2-digit numbers, only now we can trade from the hundreds too! 🤝

📚 Watch how 524 − 268 works:

Step 1 — Ones: 4 − 8? Can't! Trade 1 ten → 14 − 8 = 6.

Step 2 — Tens: Now 1 ten left. 1 − 6? Can't! Trade 1 hundred → 11 − 6 = 5.

Step 3 — Hundreds: 4 − 2 = 2.

⁴¹¹⁴  524 −268  256

✨ Answer: 256!

🥧 Fractions

Fractions of a Whole

A fraction is an equal part of a whole. The whole must be split into EQUAL pieces — same size every time! ✂️

🥧 Naming the Parts

Halves — 1/22 equal parts
Thirds — 1/33 equal parts
Fourths — 1/44 equal parts
The wholeAll the parts together

The more pieces you cut, the SMALLER each piece is. A fourth is smaller than a half!

📏 Measurement

Measuring Length

We measure how long things are with units. Small things use inches and centimeters; big things use feet and yards. 📐

📏 Know Your Length Units

Centimeter (cm)About the width of your pinky nail
Inch (in)About the length of a paper-clip — BIGGER than a cm
Foot (ft)12 inches — about the length of a shoe
Yard (yd)3 feet — about one big step
